Work
====
My most recent role was Educational Technology Manager for Texas School Ready (TSR) at Children's Learning Institute (CLI). CLI is a part of The University of Texas Health Science Center at Houston (UTHealth) located in the Texas Medical Center in Houston, Texas. My work specializations and interests include platform architecture and design, system integration, and data analytics. I served as project manager for the design and implementation of a state-wide child progress monitoring and professional development learning platform by the name of CLI Engage.

========
Research
========
I am an Ed.D. student in the Department of Curriculum & Instruction at the University of Houston. My dissertation research involves how the knowledge discovery process (KDP) relates to student retention and data extracted from the university's PeopleSoft data warehouse. My academic advisor is Dr. Sara McNeil. My research interests include data preparation, KDP, student retention, data visualization and platform architecture.

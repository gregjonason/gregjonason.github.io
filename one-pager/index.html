About

My name is Gregory Jonason and I am an expert at data manipulation. Currently, I am an Application Developer for Enterprise Systems at the University of Houston.

I am interested in converting unstructured data into structured data that can then be used by data analysts to derive insights. This task can be frustrating for most, but serves as a way to relax my mind.

Managing large and small IT projects for small to medium sized business is one of my other passions. Finally, building large Lego sculptures is another one of my skills.

If you are looking to fill any freelance work please contact me.
